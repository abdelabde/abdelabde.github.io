Abdelhakim Abdellaoui PhD Candidate in Applied Mathematics · Polytechnique Montréal

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Mathematics and Industrial Engineering at Polytechnique Montréal and a member of GERAD, supervised by Prof. Issmail El Hallaoui and Prof. Loubna Benabbou.

My research focuses on the theoretical foundations of reinforcement learning and optimization, with the goal of designing AI algorithms for combinatorial optimization problems. I am also a co-founder of the OR@Africa initiative and an ERP Functional Consultant at Arche TI.

Prior to Polytechnique, I earned an Industrial Engineering diploma from Mohammadia School of Engineering (EMI) in Rabat, Morocco.

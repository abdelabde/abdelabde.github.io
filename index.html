Abdelhakim Abdellaoui

PhD Candidate · Polytechnique Montréal · GERAD

I am a PhD candidate in Industrial Engineering at Polytechnique Montréal and a member of GERAD, supervised by Prof. Issmail El Hallaoui and Prof. Loubna Benabbou.

My research focuses on optimizing large-scale, operationally rich vehicle routing problems by combining machine learning and operations research. I develop decomposition-based frameworks, clustering-driven heuristics, and reinforcement learning approaches for real-world logistics — with current applications in Canadian forestry transportation, generating an estimated $8M/year in savings.

I am also a co-founder of OR@Africa and previously served as an ERP Consultant at Arche TI. Before Polytechnique, I earned an Industrial Engineering diploma from Mohammadia School of Engineering (EMI) in Rabat, Morocco.
